Flip

The Flip rendering lets you work with two items: the front (visible) and the back (hidden). These two items are shown alternatively after you either click or hover over the item. For example, you can use the flip rendering for staff member photographs and profile information, or for coupons and redeem codes

To configure a Flip component, follow these steps:
  1. Under Data folder:
    • Create Flip datasource
  2. Drag and drop the Flip rendering to the page
  3. In the Select the Associated Content dialog box:
    • To select an existing content item, click Select Existing Content, click the content item that you want and then click OK.
    • To create a new content item for the flip and add flip slides directly on the page, click Create New Content and click OK, and then click the plus button:
  4. In the Control Properties dialog box, in the Styling section, you can select different transition modes, such as Transition on hover and Transition - Rotate vertically.
  5. For more details, see Sitecore documentation
