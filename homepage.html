Amgen SXA Showcase is a set of components to speed up the production of websites and to reuse components across a variety of sites.

Color

Each Amgen brand features five color palettes.

Swatches show WCAG 2.0 accessible contrast rating: AA18 3:1 for large text, AA 4.5:1, and AAA 7:1.

Typography

Across brands, Amgen's typographic style maintains a consistent visual hierarchy.

Brand stylesheets may include the following overrides, which are reflected in the swatches below:

  • font-size may be adjusted from the base 16px.
